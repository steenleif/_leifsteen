Leif Steen is an independent designer based in London with the ambition to create meaningful and innovative projects. After completing an MA in Graphic Design at the London College of Communication in 2011 he freelanced for studios and companies like Browns, Pentagram, Mark Porter Associates, Wired UK and News International.


Until recently he worked at Applied Works, an award winning design and technology studio for clients such as BBC, Genesys, Aegon and others.


If you would like to speak about a project please get in touch. Services includes identity, editorial, digital and interactive design.
